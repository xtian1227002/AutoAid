About AutoAid

AutoAid is an innovative system designed to provide swift and reliable car breakdown assistance. It allows users to locate nearby auto repair shops using GPS, ensuring help is always close at hand. AutoAid maintains a detailed repair history for each vehicle, accessible by both users and repair shops. This feature ensures transparency and helps in tracking the vehicle's maintenance over time. With AutoAid, users experience peace of mind knowing their car's repair history is well-documented and assistance is just a click away.
